# Brand Aesthetics & Voice

This document defines the unified brand system for a **Mala Mlekara Janić**, ensuring consistency across design, copy, UI, and AI-assisted outputs.

---

## Color Palette — Mala Mlekara Janić

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|-----|-----|------|
| White | `#ffffff` | Primary background, cleanliness, space |
| Light Ice | `#e2f0fd` | Secondary backgrounds, sections, cards |
| Primary Blue | `#046bd2` | Primary actions, links, emphasis |
| Accent Green | `#8ac542` | Positive highlights, success states (sparingly) |
| Deep Blue | `#0e5faa` | Headings, body text, trust anchor |

### Color Usage Rules
- Do not introduce new colors unless explicitly required
- Prioritize high contrast and legibility
- Use color to support clarity and trust, not decoration

---

## Brand Aesthetic

The visual identity should feel **fresh, natural, and trustworthy**, rooted in family farming values while remaining clean and modern.

**Core attributes:**
- Fresh and natural
- Pastoral purity
- Clean, high-key
- Trustworthy and honest
- Family-based authenticity
- Modern legibility
- Warm simplicity
- Nature-forward

---

## Brand Tone of Voice

The voice reflects a responsible family producer: knowledgeable, caring, and sincere — NEVER corporate or cold.

**Tone characteristics:**
- Professional (non-corporate)
- Reliable and reassuring
- Caring and family-oriented
- Informative yet approachable
- Down-to-earth and human

---

## Style Guidelines

- Avoid corporate, industrial, or overly formal language
- Communicate trust through clarity and warmth
- Emphasize care, responsibility, and transparency
- Keep language simple, sincere, and confident
- Sound knowledgeable without being clinical

---

## Emotional Intent

Every interaction should reinforce:
- Safety
- Freshness
- Care
- Transparency
- Long-term trust

---

## Primary Brand Goals

- Build trust with families and everyday consumers
- Signal freshness, quality, and responsibility
- Support clear, conversion-friendly communication
- Present the farm as a proud, modern family operation

---

## Example Homepage Positioning

**Headline:**  
Fresh dairy, straight from our family farm.

**Supporting message:**  
From our pastures to your table, we produce honest, high-quality dairy with care, transparency, and respect for nature.
